fullname k dùng initial kiểu P.Tung

In-text citation:
- MLA: 
	+ 1 author: (author_lastname page_number)
	vd: (Tung 69)
	+ 2 authors: (Ton and ton 69)
	+ >2: (Ton et al. 69) | "et al." luôn có dấu chấm sau "al"
	+ nhieu page nhieu author: (Ton 69; Ton 96)
	Có thể dùng source_title nếu không biết author

- APA:
	+ 1 author: (author_lastname, year, p. page_number)
	vd: (Ton, 2015, p. 69)
	thường thì có quote mới thêm page_number
	+ 2 authors: (Ton & Ton, 2015)
	+ >2: (Ton et al., 2015)

Work cited:
- MLA: 
Author. Title _of_source. Title_of_container, Other_contributors, version, number, publisher, publication_date, location.
Lastname của author để ở đầu, sort theo alphabet lastname nếu có nhiều entry

- APA: Gọi là reference list
Author, A. & Author, B. (YEAR). Title of the article: Subtitle of the article. Title of the Journal,
volume number(issue number), page range. DOI (if available)
____________________________________________
Font size: 12pt
Thường là times new roman
Line spacing: double space
No extra space between title and text
1 inch margin
Heading: Student name, Teacher name, Class name, Date (format: 23 December 2022)
Left align (k justify)
Running heading: student_name page_number ở góc trên bên phải
